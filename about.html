About

Flint Landscaping has been a family owned business for over 20 years, but the name was changed to Flint Landscaping in 2007 when Jason Flint became the owner. We love to create new landscapes through garden and small tree installations, and hardscapes but we are happy to provide many services. We strive to make our clients excited about working with us, and we take great pride in our work. Flint Landscaping takes care of the details that others may overlook.
